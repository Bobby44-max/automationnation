## 3. Core "Command" Features (For Implementation)

### A. The 5 AM Autopilot
- **Function:** Automated forecast scan at 05:00 local time.
- **Action:** If thresholds (Wind/Rain/Temp/Humidity) are breached, trigger the Reschedule Engine.
- **The Payoff:** Owner wakes up to a "Crews Notified & Revenue Protected" summary.

### B. Trade-Specific Threshold Logic
- **Roofing:** Wind > 25mph | Rain > 40%
- **Painting:** Humidity > 85% | Dew Point < 5° Spread
- **Landscaping:** Rain > 60% | Soil Temp Tracking

### C. Revenue Impact Dashboard
- **Widget:** "Revenue Saved This Month" (Calculated: Number of auto-reschedules x Average Ticket Price).
- **Utility:** Proves the ROI of the subscription every single morning.
